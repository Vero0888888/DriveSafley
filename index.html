Overview

Getting your driver's license is a vital skill. It allows people to operate a class C vehicle. Also, your license can symbolize a new beginning. The small identification carries a responsibility befitting young adults. For teenagers, this is an important step in growing up. That's where we come in! The steps to obtaining your driver's license in California are tedious and confusing. With our guidance, we strive to simplify the process so you can focus on your big moment. Pathways



15-18 Years Old

Step 1

Drivers Education Class

A 30-hour course that must be completed online or in person.

Step 2

Permit Test

A 46 multiple choice question exam.

Step 3

Drivers Traning Courses

Six hours of supervised driving with a licensed instructor.

Step 4

Behind The Wheel Exam

A brief driving test overseen by a DMV test proctor.

Step 5

Probation Period

A set of driving regulations new license holders must follow.

18+ Years Old

Step 1

Permit Test

A 36 multiple choice question exam.

Step 2

Driving Practice

A period to practice your driving before being tested.

Step 3

Behind The Wheel Test

A brief driving test overseen by a DMV test proctor.
